HOW IT WORKS

  1. CREATE A PROFILE

    Describe your work experience in detail. Building a detailed profile increases your chances of getting a project.

  2. SUBMIT PROPOSALS

    Get recommended projects based on your expertise. Submit a detailed proposal to the client describing your experience in similar previous engagements.

  3. CONNECT WITH THE CLIENT

    Get shortlisted by the client and interact with the client about the project, prior to your engagement.

  4. AGREE ON MILESTONES AND DELIVERABLES

    When the client decides to engage you, agree of scope of work, milestones and deliverables.

  5. PAYMENT AND FEEDBACK

    Get paid on meeting milestones. At the end of the project, you will receive feedback from the client. Better the feedback, higher the chances of getting new projects.
